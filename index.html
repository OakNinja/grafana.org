    Time-Centric Functions

    Everything in InfluxDB is a time series that you can perform standard functions on like min, max, sum, count, mean, median, percentiles, and more.

    Scalable Metrics

    Collect your data on any interval and compute rollups on the fly later. Track 100 metrics or 1 million – InfluxDB is designed to scale horizontally.

    Events

    InfluxDB's data model supports arbitrary event data. Just write in a hash of associated data and count events, uniques, or grouped columns on the fly.
